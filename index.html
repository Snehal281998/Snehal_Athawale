Agricultural Economics Data Archive

Integrated datasets for production, trade, markets, and policy analysis

Indian Agricultural Data Platforms (Integrated Statistical System)

  • UPAg – Unified Portal for Agricultural Statistics Centralised platform integrating crop production, acreage, yield, and procurement data.
  • HAPIS – Harvest and Production Information System Real-time crop monitoring system combining remote sensing and field data for production estimates.
  • DES (Directorate of Economics & Statistics) Primary source for crop-wise area, production, and yield at state and district levels.
  • Agmarknet Market arrivals and price data across regulated mandis in India.
  • PMFBY Crop Insurance Data District-level crop coverage, yield estimates, and risk indicators.
  • Farmer Portal Advisories, crop statistics, and scheme-level agricultural information.

Global Agricultural Production Data

  • FAOSTAT Production Global crop and livestock production, yield, and area harvested (1961–present).
  • Our World in Data Cleaned long-run agricultural productivity and yield datasets.
  • USDA PSD Database Production, consumption, and trade balance for major commodities.

Trade Databases (Global & India)

  • FAOSTAT Trade Global agricultural trade flows.
  • UN Comtrade Detailed bilateral trade dataset (HS classification).
  • APEDA Agri Exchange India’s agricultural export-import database.
  • Export Import Data Bank HS-level Indian trade data.

Markets & Price Intelligence

  • Agmarknet Mandi-level arrivals and wholesale prices.
  • AMIS Global food market intelligence system.
  • IMF Commodity Prices Global commodity price time series.
