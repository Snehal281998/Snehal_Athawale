Agricultural Economics Data Archive

Curated datasets for trade, markets, policy, and empirical research

Global Agricultural Trade Databases

  • FAOSTAT Trade Comprehensive global agricultural trade flows (1961–present) across commodities and countries.
  • UN Comtrade Highly disaggregated bilateral trade data using HS/SITC classifications; essential for gravity modelling.
  • World Bank WITS Trade data combined with tariffs and non-tariff measures for policy analysis.
  • ITC Trade Map Export competitiveness indicators, market shares, and bilateral trade insights.
  • OECD Trade & Agriculture Producer support estimates and trade policy indicators across developed economies.

Indian Agricultural Trade Databases

  • APEDA Agri Exchange India’s primary dataset for commodity-wise and country-wise agricultural exports and imports.
  • DGCIS Trade Statistics Official merchandise trade data of India at aggregated and disaggregated levels.
  • India Trade Portal Tariffs, SPS measures, export procedures, and trade agreements.
  • Export Import Data Bank (MoC) HS code-based trade data for detailed commodity-level empirical analysis.
  • RBI Database (DBIE) Macroeconomic indicators including trade balance and exchange rates.

Markets, Prices & Commodity Intelligence

  • USDA ERS Global commodity outlooks, farm income, and agricultural trade projections.
  • AMIS Market intelligence for wheat, rice, maize, and soybean with trade and stock data.
  • FAO GIEWS Prices Retail and wholesale food price monitoring across countries.
  • IMF Commodity Prices Monthly international commodity prices used in price transmission studies.

Production, Macro & Structural Indicators

  • World Bank Agriculture Data Agricultural GDP, employment, and structural transformation indicators.
  • FAOSTAT Production Global crop production, land use, and food balance sheets.
  • UPAg Portal (India) Unified agricultural statistics including crop estimates and procurement data.

Policy, Surveys & Research Data

  • IFPRI Datasets Household surveys and food policy modelling datasets.
  • IndiaStat Agriculture Integrated Indian datasets on production, trade, prices, and inputs.
  • Economic Survey of India Analytical insights on agricultural trade, policy, and structural change.
